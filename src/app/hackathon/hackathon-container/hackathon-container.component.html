Fintech Hackathon 2019

One of the most challenging and innovative Hackathon of the region

Fintech Hackathon is the finest accomplishment of Momentum as we take pride in giving all tech geeks a platform to showcase their talent. We designed it to pave our way into the tech-powered tomorrow. During the event, teams gather around a huddle for 48 hours to build ideas and create solutions for it. This year it will be about finding a tech-powered solution for a real-time case from the financial sector backed by industry’s biggest names.

Hackathon Judging Criteria
Entry rules
  • Teams of 2-5 humans are allowed to compete. Individuals are welcome to join and we will try to build the teams on the spot.
  • Developers, designers and people with ideas on how to handle the challenges/tasks are allowed in the teams.
  • Only demos of those hacks are allowed in presentations. No slide-decks or power-points are allowed.
  • Presentations are limited to a 3-minute presentation and possible Q&A from the judges.
  • All hacks must be submitted by 2 pm on the second day.
